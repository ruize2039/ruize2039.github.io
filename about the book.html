About the books

Book Harry Potter and the Descriptio
1Sorcerers Stone Harry discovers he is a wizard on his eleventh birthday and has been accepted to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry
2Chamber of SecretsDespite warnings from a house-elf named Dobby, Harry returns to Hogwarts, where a mysterious "Chamber of Secrets" has been opened, unleashing a monster that petrifies students.
3Prizinor of AzkabanHarry's third year at Hogwarts, focusing on his survival against an escaped convict rather than direct confrontation with Voldemort
4Goblet of FireHogwarts hosts the Triwizard Tournament, a dangerous competition between three rival wizarding schools
5The Half Blood PrinceThe Dark Lord is looking for a prophecy in a glass sphere that only Harry can retrieve. Dumbledore's Army fights the Death Eaters until the Order of the Phoenix arrives
6Order of the Pheonixfollows Harry’s sixth year at Hogwarts, where he learns about Lord Voldemort's past and Horcruxes from Dumbledore to prepare for the final battles
7The The Deathly Hollowsfollows Harry, Ron, and Hermione on a dangerous mission to find and destroy Lord Voldemort’s remaining Horcruxes
